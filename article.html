Facts

    The Hottest Planet: Even though Mercury is closer, Venus is the hottest planet in the solar system, with surface temperatures soaring to around 460°C due to a runaway greenhouse effect.

    The Fastest Planet: Mercury is the smallest and fastest planet, zooming around the Sun in just 88 Earth days.

    The Largest Planet:Jupiter is the largest planet; it could hold over 1,000 Earths inside it, and its massive gravity acts as a shield to protect the inner solar system from wandering comets and asteroids.
